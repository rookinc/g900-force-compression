# G900 force confinement theorem candidate 001

Status: theorem candidate

Statement

A confinement shadow exists if there is a replay-stable selector family whose registered traces repeatedly and persistently exhibit bounded retention through one or more of:

- closed support
- boundary holding
- escape resistance
- internal recurrence
- finite repair

Definitions

Confinement shadow

A stable grammar of registered boundary retention.

Strong shadow

Project shorthand for a confinement shadow.

Boundary

A confinement shadow is:

- not QCD
- not color charge
- not gluons
- not physical strong force
- not body mutation
- not transport admission
- not a force renderer

Research question

Do existing G900 receipts and information transports exhibit replay-stable confinement signatures?

Success condition

At least one selector family survives replay and bounded perturbation tests while maintaining persistent bounded retention receipts.
