# G900 force compression theorem candidate 001

Status: theorem candidate

Statement

A compression shadow exists if there is a replay-stable selector family
whose registered traces repeatedly exhibit one or more of:

- inward bias
- support contraction
- reduced spread
- load concentration
- receipt-home selection

Definitions

Compression shadow

A stable grammar of registered inward concentration.

Gravity shadow

Project shorthand for a compression shadow.

Boundary

A compression shadow is:

- not mass
- not spacetime curvature
- not physical gravity
- not body mutation
- not transport admission
- not a force renderer

Research question

Do existing G900 receipts and information transports exhibit replay-stable
compression signatures?

Success condition

At least one selector family survives replay and perturbation tests while
maintaining bounded inward concentration receipts.
