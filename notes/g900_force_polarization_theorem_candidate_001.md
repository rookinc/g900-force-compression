# G900 force polarization theorem candidate 001

Status: theorem candidate

Statement

A polarization shadow exists if there is a replay-stable selector family whose registered traces repeatedly and persistently exhibit bounded oriented contrast through one or more of:

- signed separation
- paired direction
- stable opposition
- sheet distinction
- reversible contrast

Definitions

Polarization shadow

A stable grammar of registered oriented contrast.

EM shadow

Project shorthand for a polarization shadow.

Boundary

A polarization shadow is:

- not electric charge
- not electromagnetic field
- not physical EM
- not body mutation
- not transport admission
- not a force renderer

Research question

Do existing G900 receipts and information transports exhibit replay-stable polarization signatures?

Success condition

At least one selector family survives replay and bounded perturbation tests while maintaining persistent bounded oriented contrast receipts.
